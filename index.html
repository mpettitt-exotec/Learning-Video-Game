The Learning Video Game

How to play

1. Add up to 9 players by entering their names at the top of the page and clicking the green button.

2. Once you are ready to play, click the card decks to reveal the subject to teach and the learner profile.

3. Select from the video format cards at the bottom the most appropriate for this combination.

4. Explain your choice to your colleagues and feel free to have a discussion!

5. Click the card deck to stop your turn, and click again to start with the next player.

Keep going until you have completed all the combinations!
